Rektrovista

Please note: This game is still under active development. A retro-futuristic adventure coming to Xbox and Steam. Explore procedurally generated worlds inspired by the 1980s, uncover hidden tech, and meet the RektroVistians. Crafted with love and passion by a dedicated indie developer, this game invites you to step into the shoes of a brave protagonist on a quest to unlock the mysteries of the past. Encounter a diverse cast of characters, from the enigmatic Keeper of the Lockets to the mysterious Sentinel Guardians, as you unravel the rich tapestry of lore woven throughout the game. With engaging gameplay mechanics, including item collection, puzzle solving, and dynamic storytelling, every moment promises to be a thrilling adventure. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the world of indie games, this title offers an experience that will keep you hooked from start to finish. Prepare to be transported to a bygone era filled with wonder and excitement as you embark on an unforgettable journey through the realms of nostalgia. Welcome to your new favorite adventure! This game is a work in progress and currently under active development, so features and content may change as it continues to evolve.

Tone: Tragic obsession wrapped in dry wit and cigarette smoke.
