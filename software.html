Our Discord Bots\HR Tribunal Bot

Our Tribunal Bot is the ultimate HR assistant for your community. It’s designed to resolve disputes in a light-hearted, fair manner. When conflicts arise, members can submit their cases, and the bot randomly assigns “judges” from the community to review and decide on the outcome. This creates a transparent and engaging way to handle disagreements, all while fostering a sense of community and fun.

  • Comedic Conflict Resolution: Delivers randomized, humorous outcomes for community disputes, adding a fun and light-hearted twist.
  • Randomized Decisions: Acts like a coin flip, ensuring that outcomes are unpredictable and purely for entertainment.
  • Community Engagement: Encourages fun interaction and laughter, turning potential conflicts into memorable moments for the community.
