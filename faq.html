Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our games, apps, and community.

General Questions

  • Q: What platforms are your games available on?

    A: Currently, Rektrovista will be available on Xbox and Steam. Our app, Evidence: Lies and Ledgers, will be available on iOS and Android.

  • Q: How can I join the TypeNado community?

    A: You can join our Discord server or visit our forums to connect with other developers, gamers, and fans.

  • Q: Where can I purchase your games and software?

    A: All releases will be available through our online store, Steam, Xbox, or respective app stores for mobile apps.

Support Questions

  • Q: I found a bug in one of your games. What should I do?

    A: Please report any bugs through our Discord or contact page. Our team actively reviews and addresses all reports.

  • Q: Can I request features or improvements?

    A: Absolutely! We welcome suggestions via our community channels or contact form.

  • Q: How can I get early access to your games or apps?

    A: Join our Discord community or subscribe to our newsletter to receive announcements and early access opportunities.
