About TypeNado Softworks

Creating games, software, and experiences that inspire.

Who We Are

TypeNado Softworks is a close-knit group of friends brought together by creativity, collaboration, and a shared passion for bringing ideas to life. Over the years, we’ve supported each other through thick and thin, combining our diverse talents to create unique and engaging projects. Our team includes one dedicated developer, several talented designers, and a skilled sculptor. When new software or games are in development, everyone contributes — from brainstorming ideas and refining concepts to creating fan art and visual designs. Together, we turn imagination into reality, blending technical expertise with artistic vision to craft experiences that inspire and connect.

Our Mission

We strive to create software and games that challenge the norm, inspire learning, and foster strong, supportive communities. Every project is designed to engage users, spark imagination, and build meaningful connections across a diverse audience. Through innovation, creativity, and collaboration, we aim to make technology more human — crafting experiences that educate, entertain, and empower people everywhere.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Always pushing creative boundaries.
  • Community: Building inclusive and engaging experiences.
  • Quality: Delivering polished, reliable products.
  • Learning: Growing together with every project.
